How Speaker Learning Works

1. Transcribe - Upload audio and let the system detect speakers (SPEAKER_00, SPEAKER_01, etc.)

2. Assign - Map speaker labels to real names in the transcript view

3. Learn - The system extracts voice embeddings and stores them

4. Recognize - In future transcriptions, known speakers are automatically identified
